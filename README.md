# HR Analytics Dashboard 📊

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** using the **IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance Dataset**. The dashboard helps HR professionals and decision-makers analyze workforce trends, employee attrition patterns, job satisfaction levels, and other key HR metrics.

The objective of this project is to transform raw HR data into actionable insights that can support employee retention strategies and workforce planning.


---

## 🎯 Key Metrics

* **Total Employees:** 1,480
* **Attrition Rate:** 16.08%
* **Average Monthly Income:** 6.50K
* **Average Age:** 36.92 Years

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Employee Overview

* Total Employee Count
* Average Employee Age
* Average Monthly Income
* Attrition Rate Analysis

### Attrition Analysis

* Attrition by Department
* Attrition by Gender
* Attrition by Years at Company
* Distance From Home vs Attrition

### Employee Satisfaction Analysis

* Job Satisfaction vs Attrition
* Workforce Retention Trends

### Workforce Analysis

* Overtime Distribution
* Department-wise Employee Distribution
* Age Group Segmentation

### Interactive Filters

* Age Group
* Department

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Research & Development has the highest employee count among departments.
* Employees with lower job satisfaction tend to show higher attrition rates.
* A significant percentage of employees do not work overtime.
* Most attrition occurs during the early years of employment.
* Distance from home shows varying patterns across employees who left and stayed.
